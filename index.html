    Common questions
  • What is a 30 day challenge?

    • Choosing a habit and do it for 30 days straight.

  • Why 30 days?

    • This is generally speaking enough time to feel the effects of a new habit, and therefore you can discover if this is something you want to pursue or not.

  • What are the benefits?

    • Depends on the challenge. You will at least learn about yourself and what you are capable of.

  • Why should i use this website?

    • Most people doesnt complete their 30 day challenge. A big reason for that is poor planing and structure. This website fixes that issue.
