Welcome!

This website was created so you could orginize, plan and execute your 30 day challenge more efficient. Since this is your first time visiting this page, we will quickly show you how to use this application.

Step 1

Click on "Edit" to activate edit-mode.

Step 2

Write your "Challenge name" and "Challenge description" inside the blinking box. Then click on "Save" to save your changes.

Step 3

Scroll down and click on the first circle to mark/check off a day completed in your 30 Day Challenge.

Step 4

Scroll down to the blinking box, and write a description/summary about that particular challenge day. Then click "Save" to save your changes.

Final step

Click on "Details" to hide the details/challenge description. Then click on "View" to zoom out. Good luck with your 30 Day Challenge!
