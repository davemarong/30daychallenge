Investing

Learn how to invest and make your money grow. Put some money in the stock marked/funds, read and learn every day.

Most reported benefits

  • Saving more
  • Spending less money
  • Long term thinking

Running

Set aside 20-60 minutes everyday for running. Get some good shoes and find some good routes.

Most reported benefits:

  • More energy
  • Stamina increase
  • Better sleep

Sleep

Set a time for when you will go to bed and when you will wake up(between 7 and 9 hours). Even if you dont fell tired or sleepy, you still go to bed. No phone in the bed!

Most reported benefits:

  • Better sleep
  • More stable mood
  • More focused

NoFap

Cut out all porn and masturbation. There are different levels to this challenge. You could choose to not look at pictures from social media as well.

Most reported benefits:

  • Massive decrease in dopamine release
  • Testosterone increase
  • More energy

Yoga

Choose 5-10 yoga poses, reasarch how to do them properly and strecht it out!

Most reported benefits:

  • Body awereness
  • More relaxed
  • Increase in flexibility

No Junk Food

Cut out all junk food/processed food. Replace this with natural and healthy food that will fuel your body.

Most reported benefits:

  • More energy
  • More focused
  • Less sick

Read

Find some books and set a time limit between 30-60 min for each day to read. Make sure to have a place were you will not be interupted.

Most reported benefits:

  • Learning
  • Better vocabulary
  • Better writing

Nature

Go out in nature without any external stimuli. Go with some friends or alone.

Most reported benefits:

  • More relaxed
  • Better mood
  • Less sick

Meditation

Set aside som minutes (10-30 min) everyday in a place were you wont be interupted. You can use a guided meditation if you want to.

Most reported benefits:

  • Less stress
  • Better mood
  • More productive

Saving

Look at your past months spending. Find some areas were you can cut back(food, coffe, alcohol and shopping are usually places who can be improved upon) and set up a budget.

Most reported benefits:

  • More money
  • Long term thinking
  • Awareness of money spending

Screen time

Cut out all unnecessary phone use (social media, social media and social media) and set a time limit for how much screen time you have each day. At the end of each day, write down your phone time for that particular day.

Most reported benefits:

  • More social
  • Decrease in dopamine release
  • More confidence
